# Definitions

## General

**CoW**: Co-incidence of Wants, the phenomenon whereby two or more orders have common elements allowing either a partial or whole-of-trade P2P settlement.

**Gasless trading**: The ability to make a trade between assets without having to pay a transaction fee.

## Fees

**Gas fee / transaction fee**: A fee charged by the blockchain in it's native token for execution of a transaction (eg. `ETH`, `xDAI`).

**Liquidity Provider fee**: A fee charged by a liquidity provider, such as an AMM like Uniswap, for facilitating a swap against their assets.

**Protocol fee**: A fee charged to users during the settlement, paid in the sell token, as a fee for the trade being settled.
